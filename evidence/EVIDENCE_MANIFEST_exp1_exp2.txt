EVIDENCE MANIFEST — lcr-reproduction archive
=============================================
Date: 2026-06-10
Paper: "Lawful Compression and the Price of Readout: Task-Relevant
Uncertainty under Sufficiency, Interface Geometry, and Pre-Registered
Measurement" (J.W. Miller, CURV Institute)

Purpose. This manifest pins, by cryptographic digest, the evidence that
the paper's two pre-registrations were committed before their
corresponding experimental runs. The manifest itself is externally
timestamped via OpenTimestamps (file EVIDENCE_MANIFEST.txt.ots in this
repository): only the SHA-256 digest of this file leaves the machine,
and the attestation is anchored in the Bitcoin blockchain via public
calendar servers. The .ots file is created as a pending attestation,
complete at the calendar servers immediately; it upgrades to a full
Bitcoin attestation once the calendars' commitment is confirmed
on-chain (run `ots upgrade EVIDENCE_MANIFEST.txt.ots`, then
`ots verify EVIDENCE_MANIFEST.txt.ots` against this file). The same
mechanism is applied to the full-history bundle digest below
(lcr.bundle.ots in the repository root), which pins the entire commit
history of this archive — including every commit listed here, its
content, and its dates — as it existed on the manifest date.

Verification recipe (once the archive is public)
------------------------------------------------
1. ots verify EVIDENCE_MANIFEST.txt.ots   (proves this manifest existed
   no later than the attested Bitcoin block time)
2. Recreate the bundle from the archive at the HEAD above and compare
   its sha256 to the value pinned here; or verify lcr.bundle.ots
   directly against an independently obtained copy of the bundle.
3. git verify the four evidence commits and the submission-draft tag
   exist in the bundled history with the listed hashes; commit hashes
   are themselves content addresses over tree, parents, and dates.
