PRICE OF READOUT — combined evidence manifest
=============================================
Date: 2026-06-15 (UTC)
Paper: "The Price of Readout: Exact Interface Floors and Frozen-Readout
Shift Evaluation" (J.W. Miller, CURV Institute).
Repository: https://github.com/curv-institute/price-of-readout
Tag: v1.0-submission

Purpose. This manifest is the single, paper-specific evidence anchor for
the public reproduction repository. It pins (1) the pre-registration,
amendment, and results commit hashes of all three experiments, in the
repositories where those commits live, and (2) the SHA-256 digest of
every file as it sits in THIS repository. The manifest itself is
externally timestamped via OpenTimestamps (file
PRICE_OF_READOUT_EVIDENCE.txt.ots beside it): only this file's SHA-256
digest leaves the machine, anchored in the Bitcoin blockchain via public
calendar servers. Verify with
  ots verify PRICE_OF_READOUT_EVIDENCE.txt.ots
against this file (run `ots upgrade` first to pull the on-chain proof).

This anchor SUPPLEMENTS, and does not replace, the two pre-existing
attestations reproduced verbatim in evidence/: the Experiment 1 & 2
manifest (EVIDENCE_MANIFEST_exp1_exp2.txt.ots, dated 2026-06-10,
Bitcoin-confirmed) and the Experiment 3 manifest
(EXPERIMENT_EVIDENCE_MANIFEST_exp3.txt.ots, dated 2026-06-11/12). Those
attestations, predating this repository, are the before-any-run evidence;
the git dates in this repository are NOT (see evidence/PROVENANCE.md).
